### 1. **Complete the Main Story and Archon Quests**

- Focus on progressing through the main story and Archon Quests early on to unlock more regions, features, and higher Adventure Rank (AR) rewards.

### 2. **Level Up Your Adventure Rank (AR)**

- Your AR determines the level of resources you can access and the strength of enemies. Prioritize leveling up your AR by completing daily commissions, world quests, and clearing domains.

### 3. **Optimize Your Party Composition**

- Build a balanced team with different elements to take advantage of elemental reactions (e.g., Fire + Water = Vaporize). Make sure you have a DPS, a support/healer, and a utility character for exploration.

### 4. **Focus on One or Two Main Characters Early**

- Don’t spread your resources too thin. Level up and gear a few key characters to make progress faster. This helps you conserve materials and make more meaningful improvements.

### 5. **Complete Daily Commissions and Battle Pass Tasks**

- Dailies give you Primogems, Adventurer's EXP, and other valuable items. Make sure you log in daily to complete these tasks.

### 6. **Use Your Resin Wisely**

- Original Resin is valuable for farming high-level bosses, domains, and Ley Line Outcrops. Don’t waste it on low-tier resources unless needed for a specific upgrade.

### 7. **Collect Resources and Explore**

- Gather local resources for crafting, cooking, and ascensions as you explore the world. This includes ores for weapon crafting, herbs for cooking, and materials for character ascension.

### 8. **Don’t Forget to Upgrade Your Weapons**

- Weapons can make a big difference. Prioritize upgrading the best weapons for your main DPS and support characters.

### 9. **Elemental Reactions Are Key**

- Use elemental reactions to maximize your damage. Learn the basics like Melt, Electro-Charged, Superconduct, and Swirl to become more efficient in combat.

### 10. **Manage Your Resources Carefully**

- Don’t waste resources like Mora and Ascension materials on characters you don’t plan to use long-term. Prioritize leveling up characters that suit your playstyle.

### 11. **Level Up Your Artifacts**

- Artifacts significantly impact your character’s stats. Focus on farming 3-star and 4-star artifacts in domains until you can get 5-star ones. Equip artifacts that match the character’s best stats (e.g., Crit Rate, Crit DMG for DPS).

### 12. **Use the Adventurer Handbook**

- The Adventurer Handbook contains challenges and activities that reward you with valuable items like Primogems and resources. Keep an eye on this for extra bonuses.

### 13. **Participate in Events**

- Genshin Impact frequently runs events that offer Primogems, exclusive items, and other rewards. These can be a great source of resources and entertainment.

### 14. **Save Your Primogems for Banners**

- Primogems are best spent on character and weapon banners, especially if you’re aiming for a specific 5-star. Don’t spend them recklessly on single pulls.

### 15. **Pay Attention to Stamina Management**

- Your stamina is essential for running, climbing, swimming, and gliding. Make sure to manage your stamina effectively, especially in combat and exploration.

Following these tips will help you set a strong foundation and enjoy your Genshin Impact adventure. Let me know if you want more specific guidance or tips for a certain aspect of the game!
